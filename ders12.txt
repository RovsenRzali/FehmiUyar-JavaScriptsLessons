>>>>>JavaScript Comparison Operators<<<<<

* ==  equal to (beraberdir). deyer olaraq beraber olub olmadigin yoxlayir
      yeni biri string "5" biri number disa, deyer olaraq beraberliyi yoxlayir.
      dirnagin icerisindeki deyer  number-a beraber olub olmadigin yoxlayir.

* === equal value and equal type (esit deyer ve esit tur).
        hem tur hemde deyer olaraq beraber olmalidir. Eger biri ferqlidise
        sert odenmir.

* !=  not equal (beraber deil).

* !== not equal value or not equal type (beraber olmayan deyer ve ya esit olmayan tur).

* >

* <

* >=  greater than or equal to (den daha boyuk ve ya beraber)

* <=  less than or equal to (den daha kicik ve ya beraber).

* ?  ternary operator (uclu operator).
